# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its code of
conduct. The canonical text is maintained at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/> and is
incorporated here by reference.

By participating in this project — opening issues, sending pull requests,
commenting, or any other form of contribution — you agree to abide by it.

## Reporting

Reports of unacceptable behavior go to **support@logicalmechanism.io**.
Reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The reporter's
identity will be kept confidential to the extent the investigation allows.

For security issues (vulnerabilities, deanonymization attacks, etc.), see
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead. The two channels are intentionally
separate.

## Enforcement

Enforcement follows the **Community Impact Guidelines** described in the
Contributor Covenant 2.1, available at the URL above. The maintainers are
responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards and will take appropriate
and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem
inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
